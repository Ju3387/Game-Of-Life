Rules of the "Game Of Life"

  • Back

    • 1. Underpopulation: A living cell with fewer than 2 living neighbors dies.

    2. Overpopulation: A living cell with more than 3 living neighbors dies.

    3. Survival: A living cell with 2 or 3 living neighbors stays alive.

    4. A dead cell with exactly 3 living neighbors becomes alive.
